Multi-Language Support

English, Dutch, French, Russian, Danish, Italian, Spanish, Hungarian, Vietnamese, Chinese (Traditional), & Redneck

Scout-App 2 was designed with a powerful translation engine allowing for easily swapping out language files.

Would you like to help translate Scout-App?

Translating Scout-App 2 Instructions

  1. Login to your Google Account.
  2. Go to the translation file on Google Sheets.
  3. There is an en column for the English version, and a CONTEXT column that explains the usage of the text.
  4. Create a new column for your language. (Hide columns from languages you don't care about.)
  5. Translate all of the en phrases. Add another column for any notes related to your translations.
  6. Add an issue to GitHub to inform us of an update to the document.

Minimum System Requirements

Windows
At least 64MB of available RAM. At least 111MB of free space. XP, Vista, 7, 8, 10. 32-Bit or 64-Bit OS (ARM not supported).
At least 64MB of available RAM. At least 159MB of free space. 10.6, 10.7, 10.8, 10.9, 10.10, 10.11. 64-Bit OS only (32-Bit and ARM are not supported).
